The editorial

Why this one was kept.

Two hundred and fourteen properties were considered for the index. Thirty-one survived. Soneva Jani stayed for four reasons, with one honest weakness we name up front, because you would notice it anyway.

The largest overwater villas

From 411 m² to 1,664 m², with retractable roofs that open the bedroom to the sky. The waning moon this week makes the timing right.

The quiet is structural

Twenty-four villas across a 5.6 km lagoon. The nearest neighbour is far enough that you will not hear them. We measured.

The only observatory in the country

A roll-back roof, a resident astronomer, and the clearest night skies in the Maldives. Nowhere else has one.

An honest weakness: the reef

The lagoon is sand, not coral. Serious snorkelling is a 20-minute boat ride. If the house reef matters most, see Vakkaru, number 7 in the index.
