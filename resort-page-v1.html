The editorial

Why this one was kept.

214 properties were considered for the index. 31 survived. The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli stayed for 4 reasons, with 1 honest weakness we name up front, because you would notice it anyway.

Architecture with a signature

WOW Architects shaped the whole island — most of all The Whale Bar, a sweeping timber shell over the water that has become the resort’s emblem.

The Iridium Spa & Blue Hole

An overwater spa reached by its own bridge, built around a marine Blue Hole hydrotherapy pool. Few spas in the Maldives match its scale.

The St. Regis Butler

A 24-hour butler to every villa, in the house tradition — the unpacking, the evening ritual, the things you would rather not ask twice for.

An honest weakness: it is polished, not barefoot

This is grand, design-led luxury rather than castaway simplicity. If you want bohemian and sand-between-the-toes, see Soneva, number 1 in the index.
