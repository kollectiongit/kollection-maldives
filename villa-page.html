The reading

The cheapest way onto the water here — and the one we send most couples to.

You're paying for the deck, not the square metres. At 130 m² the interior sits mid-pack for Vommuli, but the pool runs right to the lagoon edge with a ladder into water clear to the sand. It faces east, so mornings are the event — for sunset from your own deck, step one category up to the Sunset Overwater. Butler service is assigned before you land, which at this price point is the real story.

Kollection benchmark · Value on the water: 1st of 6 overwater categories · Space: mid-pack · Source: 2026 Vommuli survey visit

Why this one

Straight into the reef

Steps off the deck reach living coral, not just sand — unusual this close to a villa, and the reason snorkellers book it over the beach villas.

Yours faces the sunrise

We flag this plainly: the deck is east-facing. Perfect for coffee at first light; if you want the sun setting into your own pool, the Sunset Overwater is the honest pick.

A butler from touchdown

The St. Regis butler is assigned before arrival — unpacking, pressing, the floating breakfast, the evening drink poured on the deck. Included, not a surcharge.
