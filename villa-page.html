Two-Bedroom Sunset Overwater Villa with Pool

★ 5.0 · The Kollection reading · Vommuli Island, South Nilandhe Atoll

A double overwater villa facing the sunset — two ensuite suites, a long pool deck and an uninterrupted western horizon, for two couples or a family who won't trade the view.

4 guests 2 bedrooms Private pool 280 m² Sunset · west-facing

The reading

The island's sunset seat, with a second suite — for two couples or a family who won't share a wall.

At $5,600 it's nearly double the one-bedroom Sunset Overwater ($2,900), and the sunset isn't what you're paying for — you'd get the same western horizon there. You're buying the second ensuite suite and the length of the pool deck. Against the beach-set Two-Bedroom Ocean Villa ($4,800), this is an ~$800 premium to be over open water with nothing between the deck and the horizon. Two full suites means nobody takes the sofa.

Kollection benchmark · Sunset view: best-in-class · vs 1-BR Sunset: +$2,700 for the 2nd suite · Source: 2026 Vommuli survey visit

Why this one

Two suites, no compromise

Two separate ensuite bedrooms, each with its own bath and wardrobe — for two couples or a family who don't want to draw straws over the master.

The sunset is yours

West-facing over open water — the sun sets into your pool deck, not behind a neighbour. It's the one reason to choose this over the (cheaper) beach-set two-bedroom.

A butler from touchdown

The St. Regis butler is assigned before arrival — unpacking, pressing, the floating breakfast, the evening drink poured on the deck. Included, not a surcharge.
